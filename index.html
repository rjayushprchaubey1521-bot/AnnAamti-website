1. Freshness & Safety

Ann-Amrit serves food without harmful preservatives. Food must be consumed within 2 hours of delivery.

2. Validity, Extensions & Emergencies

A 30-day meal plan includes a maximum normal extension of 04 days. Further extensions are strictly granted only for genuine emergencies upon mandatory submission of valid documentary proof (Admit Card, Travel Ticket, Medical Receipt).

To pause a meal, inform us via App/WhatsApp at least 12 hours in advance. Uninformed skips will be counted as consumed.
