Who We Are

AnnAamrit is not just a food brand — it is a revival of India’s lost food culture. In today’s fast-moving world, food has become convenient but disconnected from purity. AnnAamrit stands as a reminder that Bharat was, is, and will always remain an “Annapurna Desh”.

Built from real-life struggles — not luxury setups. Students lacked healthy food, professionals compromised health, and families lost trust in outside meals. From Day 01, our mission was clear: to create food people can trust without doubt.

Who We Serve

  • Students: Affordable, clean, energy-rich meals.
  • Working Professionals: Time-saving, reliable food.
  • Families: Safe and home-style meals.
  • Health-Conscious: Sattvic and disciplined diet.

1. Freshness & Safety

Ann-Amrit serves food without harmful preservatives. Food must be consumed within 2 hours of delivery.
ताज़गी बनाए रखने के लिए डिलीवरी के 2 घंटे के भीतर भोजन ग्रहण करना अनिवार्य है।

2. Validity, Extensions & Emergencies

A 30-day meal plan includes a maximum normal extension of 04 days. Further extensions are strictly granted only for genuine emergencies upon mandatory submission of valid documentary proof (Admit Card, Travel Ticket, Medical Receipt).
अतिरिक्त विस्तार केवल आपात स्थिति (परीक्षा, यात्रा, चिकित्सा) में वैध प्रमाण दिखाने पर ही मान्य होगा।

3. Pause Policy

To pause a meal, inform us via App/WhatsApp at least 12 hours in advance. Uninformed skips will be counted as consumed.
भोजन रोकने के लिए 12 घंटे पहले सूचित करना अनिवार्य है।

4. Code of Conduct

Customers are requested to maintain respectful behavior with staff. Misbehavior may result in immediate termination without refund.
स्टाफ के साथ सम्मानजनक व्यवहार अपेक्षित है।
