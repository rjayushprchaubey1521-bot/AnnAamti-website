The Mother's Touch

AnnAamrit was not born in a boardroom; it was born from a very real family need. When our family moved from Bettiah, West Champaran to Patna for better education, the most basic daily problem became obvious: good, pure food was missing. Outside food created struggle and health issues.

That is when our mother, Rubi Devi, stepped in. She began cooking with love, and her home-style food naturally created a deep emotional response. Neighbors and students living nearby noticed, and they too craved the comfort of real home cooking. What started as lunchboxes for nearby students has grown into a cultural food movement.

Today, AnnAamrit stands as Patna's premier food ecosystem. We promise food that heals, nourishes, and restores the trust of 'Ghar ka khana'.
